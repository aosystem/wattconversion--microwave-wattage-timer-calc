## 🌟 The Problem & The Solution
Most frozen food or recipe instructions are based on specific wattages (e.g., 500W). If your microwave is 600W, 700W, or 1000W, simply following the label can lead to dry or unevenly cooked food. This app provides a precise mathematical conversion to give you the ideal duration instantly.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Instant Conversion:** Enter the source wattage, source time, and your target wattage to get the recalculated time immediately.
- **Mathematical Precision:** Uses an energy-based calculation formula ($W_1 \times T_1 = W_2 \times T_2$) for consistent results.
- **Intuitive Workflow:** A clean, minimal interface designed for quick use while you are busy in the kitchen.
- **Cooking Optimization:** Helps maintain food texture by preventing excessive moisture loss.
- **Global Support:** Fully localized in **50+ languages**, supporting kitchen appliances worldwide.

## 🛠 Technical Overview
- **Framework:** Flutter
- **Language:** Dart
- **Logic:** Implementation of energy conservation principles to determine time-to-power ratios, wrapped in a responsive, real-time UI.
